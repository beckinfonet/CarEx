<mechanism_decision>
Chosen mechanism: (a) Add an optional `leading?: ReactNode` prop to `FloatingSearchPill`, rendered inside its existing padded wrapper, with the pill itself wrapped in `flex: 1` so it shrinks to make room.

Why (a) over (b): The pill's outer wrapper (`paddingHorizontal: 18, paddingTop: 12`) is the element that sits directly under SafeAreaView and provides the sticky-above-FlatList behavior. Keeping the leading slot INSIDE that wrapper preserves the sticky behavior automatically and keeps all horizontal spacing centralized in ONE place — no risk of double horizontal padding, no new row container in the screen. The wrapper becomes a `flexDirection: row` with the leading slot + the pill (flex:1).
</mechanism_decision>
